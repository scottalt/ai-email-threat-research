Core Question: What should happen when an unsigned-in player reaches the Play button?

Pick the general direction — we'll refine the details next

A

Move Sign-In Next to Play

Restructure the mobile layout so the sign-in card appears directly above the Play button, not at the top of the page. The gate is always visible next to the action.

B

Let Them Play Without Signing In

Remove the sign-in gate entirely. Let anyone tap Play to start a game. Show a "sign in to save your score" prompt after the round ends (or inline during gameplay). Reduce friction to zero.

C

Sticky Sign-In Banner

When the user scrolls past the sign-in card, show a compact sticky bar at the top of the screen: "Sign in to play · [Enter Email]". Tapping Play also scrolls up to the sign-in form with a visual highlight.

D

Inline Auth on Play Tap

Keep the current layout, but when an unsigned-in user taps Play, expand an email input directly below the Play button (instead of scrolling up or showing nothing). The auth form comes to them.
