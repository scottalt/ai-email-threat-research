Inline Auth: Phrasing Options

The line that needs to work for both new and returning players

A

"Pick up where you left off — or start fresh"

Explicit dual-audience. Returning players see themselves in the first half, new players in the second.

B

"New analyst or returning? Same door."

Fits the terminal/hacker theme. Acknowledges both groups with personality. "Same door" removes any worry about picking the wrong option.

C

"Your email is your key — new or returning"

Focuses on the mechanic (email = identity) rather than the audience. Implies you don't need to remember a password or create an account.

D

"Enter your callsign to continue"

In-universe language. Doesn't distinguish new vs returning — treats everyone the same. "Continue" works whether you're continuing from round 12 or starting round 1.

E

No subtitle — let the bullet points do the work

Skip the dual-audience line entirely. The value props (Track XP, research, unlocks) already explain why to sign in. The email field is self-explanatory. Less text = less friction.
